About flexitank

Flexitank is a durable multilayer polyethylene liner placed in a standard container. This technology is used to transport bulk cargo, and the container volume is used to the maximum. The characteristics of flexitanks allow you to transport any non-hazardous liquid cargo.

Flexitanks are made of highly durable four-layer synthetic fiber (three inner and one outer layer). Patented surface design prevents the flexitank from being displaced during transportation.

Flexitank material
Flexitanks are made of highly durable four-layer synthetic fiber (three inner and one outer layer). Patented surface design prevents the flexitank from being displaced during transportation.
Protective separation
The walls of the container are lined with corrugated fiberboard to protect the flexitank from mechanical damage and prevent direct contact of the flexitank with container walls.
Stiffening edg
The container has additional stiffening steel edges to protect both the container and the flexitank during transportation.
Hose with valve
The pipe for pouring liquids with a valve. The 3-inch-diameter CAM-LOCK C300 hose with throttle valve and space for a seal. The valve is in the lower part, allowing you to minimize waste when pouring out.
Air valve
An automatic air valve for letting out air while pouring and transporting the liquid, leading to complete security and impermeability of the flexitank.

What you avoid with our flexitanks

Stolen liquid and customs checks
